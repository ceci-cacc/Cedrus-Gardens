OurPhilosophy

Sustainable design

Cedrus Gardens want's to keep our natural landscapes looking beautiful for many years to come. Xeriscaping is a philosophy about creating outdoor spaces that minimize water consumption by using native, dought-resistent plant in each design. Xeriscaping also helps to reduce the need for pesticides and herbicides as we work with the natural enviroment of your home to create a sustainable space.
